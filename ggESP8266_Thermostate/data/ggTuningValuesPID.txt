===============

Closed Loop at Oscillation
--------------------------

Ku: "ultimate" gain, when system starts oscilalting
Tu: oscillation period at Ku

Kp = 0.6 * Ku
Ki = 1.2 * Ku / Tu
Kd = 3 * Ku * Tu / 40

Open Loop Step Response
-----------------------

T: Rise time of tangent at inflection point
L1: Dead time

Kp = 1.2 * T / L1
Ti = 2 * L1
Td = L1 / 2

Ki = 1 / Ti = 0.5 / L1
Kd = 1 / Td = 2 / L1
